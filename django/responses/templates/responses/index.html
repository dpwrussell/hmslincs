pERK response to FGF-1 vs. pAKT response to IGF-1 (over time)

The pERK response to FGF-1 (y-axis) occurs early, while the pAKT response to IGF-1 occurs at the last time point. In general, the Her2amp cell lines (triangles) that are the most responsive to Lapatinib (in red) are the weakest responders to FGF-1 or IGF-1.

Basal pErbB2 vs. ErbB2

Basal expression levels and phosphorylation levels of ErbB2 are strongly correlated. The cell lines responsive to Lapatinib (red) are the ones with the highest levels of ErbB2 which are mainly Her2amp (triangles).

Ligand responses

pERK responses, to EGF vs. BTC

Average pERK fold-change response to BTC and EGF are strongly correlated, suggesting strong similarities in the response to ErbB ligands targeting similar receptors. The Her2amp cell lines (triangle) are the least responsive cell lines.

pAKT response, to HRG vs. EGF

Average pAKT fold-change response to EFG and HRG are not correlated, showing that HRG is significantly different from other ErbB ligands. The HR+ cell lines (squares) being responsive only to HRG.

pERK response vs. pAKT response, to FGF-1

FGFs predominantly signal to pERK, but not to pAKT. Cells are responding by pERK (y-axis) to stimulation by FGF-1, but pAKT (x-axis) is hardly increased.

pERK response vs. pAKT response, to IGF-1

IGFs signal predominantly to AKT. Simulation with IGF-1 activates more pAKT (x-axis) than pERK (y-axis). In general, the Her2amp cell lines (triangle) are the least responsive cell lines.

Basal protein levels

Basal pAKT vs. AKT

Basal expression levels and phosphorylation levels of AKT are not correlated. The Her2amp cell lines (triangle) are generally high in pAKT.

Basal pErbB2 vs. pAKT

The Her2amp cell lines (triangle) are generally high in pErbB2 and also have a high pAKT basal level. The HR+ (squares) and TNBC (circles) have low pErbB2 basal levels, but diverse pAKT basal levels.

Basal pErbB2 vs. ErbB2

The Her2amp cell lines (triangle) are generally high in ErbB2 and also have a high pErbB2 basal level. The HR+ (squares) and TNBC (circles) have low ErbB2 basal levels and are also low in pErbB2 levels.

Basal pErbB3 vs. pErbB2

Basal levels of pErbB3 are generally below the detection threshold expect for a majority of Her2amp cell lines (triangles) that have high pErbB2.
